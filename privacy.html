Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Doublo is developed and operated by Sviara. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the app and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Ads data. Doublo uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (advertising ID), IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

Local data only. Your scores, game history, and preferences are stored on your device only and never sent to Sviara or any server.

No account required. Doublo does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to show ads. You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings:
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

Children’s Privacy

Doublo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
