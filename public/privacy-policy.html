Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Doublo ("we", "our", or "us") is a puzzle game developed by Sviara (sviara.com). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Doublo mobile application.

Information We Collect

Doublo does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play. We do not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

Advertising

Doublo displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain information to show relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage data and interaction with ads

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation on Android).

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Any data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's own data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Doublo is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
